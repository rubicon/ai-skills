## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-18

### Added
- Initial release: forensic, source-grounded work-history and client-proof research workflow.
- Three-pass workflow (discovery → targeted verification → final assembly) with up-front mode selection.
- Five entry dimensions; project-type concept clusters; genre / artifact-role / file-format / evidence-tier classification; metric taxonomy.
- Conservative evidence standards: source-aware, no invented details, employer-side vs agency/client-side separation, quarantine of unsupported claims, later-corrections-override.
- Structured outputs: evidence database, watchlist, quarantine/alias tables, resume bullet bank, case-study ranking, fully-quoted CSV source appendix, chunking.
